# Artefact.ing

## Version History
- v0.1 - 15 Jun 2025 - Initial project creation
## Core Concept
Artefact is a personal website dedicated to showcasing the creative output of Aidan O'Brien. It serves as a digital portfolio, presenting his work across various mediums including music, production, sound design, writing, and sound engineering. The site emphasizes a minimalist design philosophy, featuring black text on a white background, and is structured to reflect the ongoing nature of creative work.
## Tags
#Creative
## Guiding Principles & Intentions
- Minimalism in design, focusing on content
- Showcasing the breadth of creative output in an organized manner
- Emphasizing the perpetual process of creation ("ing" in the name indicates ongoing work)
## Key Features & Functionality
- **Landing Page**: Explains the concept behind the name "Artefact" and includes biographical information about Aidan O'Brien.
- **Words**: A blog section for written posts.
- **Sounds**: Features a music player with tracks Aidan has worked on.
- **Pictures**: Contains videos of songs and adverts with Aidan's music, each accompanied by titles and descriptions.
- **Scrapbook**: A collection of links to various projects, apps, and miscellaneous work Aidan has contributed to.
- **Postcards**: A contact page for reaching out to Aidan.
- **Dynamic Content Management**: A comprehensive back-end system for managing content, powered by a MySQL database.
## Architecture & Structure
- Front-end: Minimalist design with a focus on usability and aesthetics.
- Back-end: MySQL for dynamic content management.
